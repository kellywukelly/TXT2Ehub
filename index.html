How it reads your files

01

Trim & sniff

Stray filler bytes some sites prepend are stripped first, then a BOM check and strict UTF-8 validation run before anything else.

02

Score & decode

Legacy encodings — Big5, GBK, GB18030, Shift-JIS — are scored by valid characters minus garbage and private-use glyphs; the cleanest wins.

03

Find the chapters

Headings like 第一章 or Chapter 4 become a real table of contents, each its own XHTML page inside the EPUB.

04

Pack the EPUB

A spec-valid EPUB 3 container is zipped in-browser — mimetype stored first, then opened by any reader.
